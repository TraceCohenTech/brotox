### Pattern applied to all rewrites:
1. metaTitle opens with a specific number in the first 6 words
2. description opens with a number or metric in the first 6 words
3. Titles promise a concrete deliverable (rules, timeline, guide)
4. Descriptions create specificity that generic titles lacked
